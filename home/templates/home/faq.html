Frequently Asked Questions

Shopping at Classic Confectionary is really easy. As you look through our assorted sweets for any occasion you can click to add items to your cart. When you've finished shopping you simply proceed to the checkout where we will take all the details we need from you to process your order. Once you have successfully placed your order you will receive an order confirmation email from us.

Orders placed before 1pm Mon-Fri (working days only) are usually dispatched the same day.

If you experience any difficulties using the site, whether you're buying or just looking, please do let us know and we'll look into it.

Delivery charges are at cost price or cheaper, ensuring you get maximum value for money. For full details of our delivery rates see our delivery information page

When packing our products we make every attempt to ensure that they will arrive in pristine condition by packaging them well. We use strong postal boxes, shock-absorbing space filler and protective sheaths for goods in glass jars. If any of your products are broken/damaged and cannot be used then notify us within seven working days of receipt.

Upon receipt, to ensure your purchases remain in the very best condition, we strongly recommend that you follow the specific storage instructions for each individual product and consume within the best before date.

If your product is temporarily out of stock we will let you know how long it will be before it's available with the option to choose an alternative or cancel your order. If you want to wait for your chosen item then we will hold your order until it can be sent in its entirety.

All ingredients are listed in the individual products descriptions. Due to the wide range of products we stock, imported from around the world, we cannot guarantee that our products are free of nuts or nut traces.

All prices include VAT at the current rate of 20%. You will see a full VAT breakdown on your confirmation email, and a VAT receipt is available by request - simply email us with your order number.

We take the greatest effort to ensure the highest possible standards of online security.

We use Stripe Payment Systems to process card payments from customers. We do not store any card information on our systems or at our premises. Stripe are a world leading Secure Payment Service providers and have been accredited by the UK's leading high street banks. Stripe are leaders in the provision of safe, secure online transactions.

All card payments are made to Classic Confectionary Ltd. This is the name you will see on your credit card or bank statement.
