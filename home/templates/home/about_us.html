About Us
Classic Confectionery

A newly established, online sweet company founded in 2020 by Augustine Evanson. With a mission to serve customers their favorite childhood sweets. In the wake of the 2019 pandemic Augustine wanted to start a business that could be insulated from a new, ever changing, climate, whilst also wanting to bring comfort back into the lives of people. Thus, Classic Confectionary was born.

Catering to Millennials and for some of the early ones, now their children as well, we strive to stock childhood favorites of the highest quality. At great value for money. All packaged in sustainable material, with tracked, free delivery available on orders.

Classic Confectionary now finds itself in a dedicated warehouse and packing facility with a team of enthusiastic individuals. Being of the 'internet generation' we're aware that the Woolworths pick & mix section is no longer the place to go for a sweet treat (This setup definitely would't fly in the new socially distanced era). Everything is online now and delivered almost instantly and our aim is to bring those sweet treats to your door whilst protecting customers data and adhering to the highest hygiene standards and we're proud to make that happen!

Being a relatively new business, our range of products is rapidly changing and always expanding, be sure to follow our socials and subscribe to the newsletter below to keep in the loop and be a part of our sweet sales and promotions.

All the best,

The Classic Confectionary Team.
