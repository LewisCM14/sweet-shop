Delivery Information

Standard UK Delivery

We deliver to ALL areas of the UK.

When calculating order weight / delivery charges, a weight of 100g is added to allow for the packaging and adequate protection of the items.

We dispatch orders Monday-Friday (working days only). Your order should be delivered within 1-5 working days from the dispatch date.

All orders under 1kg are sent via Royal Mail Packet Post service, this is a non-trackable service which takes between 2-4 days to arrive from date of despatch. Orders over 1kg are sent by Royal Mail's Tracked service, this is a fully trackable service and you will receive information regarding your delivery by text or email directly from Royal Mail.

Isle of Man & Channel Islands Delivery

When calculating order weight / delivery charges, a weight of 100g is added to allow for the packaging and adequate protection of the items.

We dispatch orders Monday-Friday (working days only). Your order should be delivered within 4-10 working days from the dispatch date.

Unfortunately due to Restrictions on Royal Mails Click & Drop service we are unable to ship to BFPO locations.
