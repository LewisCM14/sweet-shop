Delivery Information

Standard UK Delivery

We deliver to ALL areas of the UK.

When calculating order weight / delivery charges, a weight of 100g is added to allow for the packaging and adequate protection of the items.

We dispatch orders Monday-Friday (working days only). Your order should be delivered within 1-5 working days from the dispatch date.

All orders under 1kg are sent via Royal Mail Packet Post service, this is a non-trackable service which takes between 2-4 days to arrive from date of despatch. Orders over 1kg are sent by Royal Mail's Tracked service, this is a fully trackable service and you will receive information regarding your delivery by text or email directly from Royal Mail.

Orders over £50 will qualify for FREE DELIVERY on a standard service

Isle of Man & Channel Islands Delivery

When calculating order weight / delivery charges, a weight of 100g is added to allow for the packaging and adequate protection of the items.

We dispatch orders Monday-Friday (working days only). Your order should be delivered within 4-10 working days from the dispatch date.

All orders under 1kg are sent via Royal Mail Packet Post service, this is a non-trackable service which takes between 4-10 days to arrive from date of despatch. Orders over 1kg are sent by Royal Mail's Tracked service, this is a fully trackable service and you will receive information regarding your delivery by text or email directly from Royal Mail.

Unfortunately due to Restrictions on Royal Mails Click & Drop service we are unable to ship to BFPO locations.

Overseas Delivery Services

Please be aware that overseas orders are subject to extra delivery costs which are calculated dependant upon location and order weight.

These extra costs will be calculated after initial order confirmation and an invoice will be sent to the email address provided, no order will be shipped until this invoice is paid in full.

Refunds will be issued within 14 working days automatically, minus processing charges, if the invoice is not paid. Please open an inquiry from within the Contact Us section if you are unable to meet the extra delivery costs and would like to initiate a refund at an earlier date.

Sorry for any inconvenience.
